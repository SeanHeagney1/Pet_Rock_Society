Sedimentary

Now let's take a look at some sedimentary rocks. These type of rocks are a lot more relaxed in manner, having had a lot of time to meditate.

Alabaster

Alabaster is a common name, not a geological name, for massive gypsum rock. It is a translucent stone, usually white, that is used for sculpture and interior decorations. Alabaster pet rocks know their own beauty, which shows in their behaviour. Very happy when displayed.

Sandstone

Sandstone is made of sand grains (0.05mm to 2mm) that may have been deposited in the sea, by rivers, or in deserts, and later cemented together by minerals precipitated from groundwater. These Pet rocks are relatively fragile so watch what you say to them.
